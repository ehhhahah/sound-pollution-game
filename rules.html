Game Overview

The Sound Pollution Game is designed to test your ability to identify different types of sound pollution in your environment. The game consists of two main phases: listening and guessing.

Game Phases

Phase 1: Listening (30 seconds)

  • Multiple sound pollution samples will play simultaneously
  • Sounds will play in a loop during this phase
  • Pay attention to identify as many different sounds as possible
  • You can adjust your listening time using the time adjustment buttons

Phase 2: Guessing (10 seconds)

  • Sounds will stop playing
  • You must identify the sounds you heard from the available options
  • Click on the sounds you believe were playing
  • Click "Apply Guess" when you're done or wait for the timer to end

Scoring System

  • Points are awarded based on the amplitude (loudness) of correctly identified sounds
  • Quieter sounds are worth more points (up to 100 points)
  • Very loud sounds have a minimum point value of 10 points
  • Incorrect guesses result in a 20-point penalty

Time Adjustment Scoring

  • Decreasing time by 10 seconds: +50 points bonus
  • Increasing time by 10 seconds: -25 points penalty
  • Time cannot be reduced below 10 seconds
  • Time cannot be increased above 60 seconds

Game Controls

  • Start Game: Begins a new game session
  • Time Adjustment Buttons: Available during the listening phase
  • Sound Buttons: Click to identify sounds during the guessing phase
  • Apply Guess: Submit your sound identifications
  • Play Again: Start a new game after completion

Tips for Success

  • Focus on identifying quieter sounds first as they're worth more points
  • Be careful with your guesses - wrong answers cost points
  • Consider the time adjustment trade-off: less time means more points, but less time to listen
  • Remember that sounds can overlap, so listen carefully for multiple sources

Game Over

  • The game ends after the guessing phase
  • Your final score is displayed
  • A list of all sounds that were played in the session is shown
  • You can start a new game using the "Play Again" button
